Hi, and welcome to the AnyBoardJS Color Quiz!

To start playing, click next.

Step 1

To start playing:

  1. Turn on your AnyBoard token
  2. Discover the token by clicking the discover button below
  3. Click to connect to the token
  4. Click next

Step 2

How the game works:

  1. A question appears
  2. Place the token on the corresponding alternative
  3. The answer will be shown, and you get a point if you guessed correctly
  4. Place your token back on the black tile for a new question
